# Interior Design Portfolio – Eleanor Yuan

**Interior Designer & Visualization Specialist**  
*Based in Jinan, China*

---

## About Me

I am an interior designer with over 10 years of professional experience, holding a Bachelor of Arts in Art Design from Shandong University, China. My work focuses on creating thoughtful, functional, and visually compelling spaces across a wide range of project types.

I have completed interior design projects for more than 10 clients across 5 countries, delivering services from initial concept through to construction documentation and furniture sourcing.

---

## Services

- **Concept Design & Space Planning** – Logical circulation and spatial relationships for any typology
- **3D Visualization** – High-quality renderings (interior & exterior) with a focus on lighting and atmosphere
- **Construction Documentation** – Detailed, coordinated drawing sets ready for permitting and contractor use
- **FF&E Sourcing & Procurement Advice** – Furniture, materials, and finishes selection within budget

---

## Project Types

I have extensive experience in:

- Commercial Spaces – bars, billiard halls, restaurants, cafes, libraries, retail showrooms, beauty salons, internet cafes
- Office Spaces – corporate interiors, co-working, and private offices
- Hospitality – boutique hotels, boutique hospitality projects
- Residential – apartments, villas, and private homes
- Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling – professional renovation and design

---

## Design Philosophy

I believe in balancing **budget with visual impact**, keeping hard finishes simple while letting soft furnishings tell the story – always with the goal of creating a comfortable, livable space that reflects the client's needs and personality.
